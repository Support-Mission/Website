Request Assistance

We prioritize safety-critical needs for households with verified financial hardship and a clear plan for ongoing upkeep.

Eligibility checklist

  • Valid Texas driver's license and current insurance
  • Proof of residency in Montgomery County area
  • Proof of income or circumstances (e.g., unemployment, student status)
  • Vehicle title/registration in your name (for repairs) or willingness to transfer (for gifts)

What to expect

  1. Apply online and upload documents.
  2. Vetting & scheduling: we confirm eligibility and schedule an inspection.
  3. Repairs or vehicle match: safety-first scope, parts sourcing, volunteer scheduling.
  4. Pickup with walkthrough and care plan.
